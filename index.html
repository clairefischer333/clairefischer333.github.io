My Bio

I grew up in Kaukauna, Wisconsin. I do not love Wisconsin because I do not find that there is enough to do, and I hate the cold. The one pro to living here is the four seasons. I love hanging out with friends and family. I grew up playing sports in middle school and high school. I played basketball, softball, and volleyball. I graduated high school planning on attending UW-Oshkosh with the intention of majoring in Medical Imaging. I unenrolled from UWO at the beginning of my second semester of my first year. I did a job shadow and passed out, so I had to make a few changes in my life regarding my future career and college education. I am now at FVTC for Web Design and Software Development. I am currently a waitress at a restaurant in Kimberly. Lastly, I love traveling, and I hope to move to Arizona one day.
